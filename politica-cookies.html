Cookies policy



What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device to collect standard internet information and visitor behavior information. When you visit this website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology. For more information, visit www.allaboutcookies.org or www.youronlinechoices.eu.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies. You can also delete all cookies that are already on your device on your browser settings. Each browser is different, search your browser documentation to learn the correct way to modify your cookies preferences.
