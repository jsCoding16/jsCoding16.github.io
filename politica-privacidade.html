Your data protection rights under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), individuals have specific rights regarding their personal data. Although this website does not collect or store your data, you generally hold the following rights:

Right of access & rectification:

You can request information about stored data and correct any inaccuracies.

Right to erasure (To be forgotten):

You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.

Right to restrict or object to processing:

You have the right to limit or object to how your data is handled.

Right to data portability:

You can request the transfer of your data to another organization or directly to you.

Right to lodge a complaint:

You have the right to complain to a data protection supervisory authority (such as CNPD in Portugal).
